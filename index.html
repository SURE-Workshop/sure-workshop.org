Welcome to SURE!

SURE is a workshop focused on advancing the science of software reverse engineering and improving how we understand complex binaries and source code. Join us to share insights, tools, and methods that help unravel the toughest code.

Important Dates

  • Paper submission deadline: July 15, 2025
  • Author notification: August 20, 2025
  • Camera-ready deadline: September 10, 2025
  • Workshop date: October 5, 2025

Call for Papers

We welcome submissions on all topics related to reverse engineering, software comprehension, decompilation, static and dynamic analysis, and human-in-the-loop tooling. More details coming soon!
